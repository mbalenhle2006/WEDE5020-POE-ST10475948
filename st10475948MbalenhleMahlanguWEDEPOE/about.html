Who We Are

Green Harvest is a nonprofit organization dedicated to sustainable farming, food security, and rural development. Our goal is to empower small-scale farmers and communities through education, resources, and advocacy.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual has access to nutritious food and sustainable farming practices are the foundation of thriving communities.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have supported numerous farmers, launched community gardens, and provided essential resources to those in need. Through training and innovation, we continue to make a lasting impact on food security.
